Accordion

The new music sharing app

Are you tired of being the only one you know who
listens to a certain band? Do you wish you could find
other people to talk about your favorite bands?
Well with this app you can!

Establish favorite musicians

Choose from the many artists listed to create a collection of your favorite artists

Create your profile

View your collection of artists and songs. Add images, one for your profile and the other for your cover photo. Share a little bio, so other can get to know a little about your music preferences.

Meet new people

Connect with others who have the same music preferneces as you do. Message other music lovers through our secure message programming.
